Most productivity systems optimize for **busyness**, not **impact**.

  

### Anti-Gravity Principle

Reduce friction for important work.

Increase friction for shallow work.

  

### My Workflow

- Morning deep work blocks

- Architecture-first task lists

- Async communication by default

  

### Key Insight

> Energy is finite. Attention is fragile.

  

Protect both aggressively.
